Bio / Experience

Ryan Nelson, 23, is currently one of the most youngest and national acclaimed freelance journalist in the United States. He covers personalities and topics in the music, sports, politics, lifestyle and current events.

Among the personalities Nelson has interviewed are: Nick Cannon, Kevin Hart, Roger Bobb, Morris Chestnut, Nia Long, Sanaa Lathan, Estelle, Waka Flocka, Keith David, Anthony Hamilton, Kirk Franklin ,B.O.B, Adrieen Bailon, Anthony Anderson, Bill Duke, Omari Hardwick, Jeff Friday, James Fortune, Anquan Boldin and many more. Among the live events that Nelson has covered are: Jazz in The Gardens , The African American Film festival, The 2013 & 2015 Tom Joyner Family Reunion, multiple red carpet movie screenings, sports events and many more. Nelson has also interned at radio station WEDR 99 JAMZ in South Florida and national television network TV One

Ryan D. Nelson was born April 10, 1992 in Los Angeles, California. His parents are Peabody Award-winning Broadcaster Carl Nelson, who is currently host of Radio One’s "The Carl Nelson Show" and Television Producer Lynda Hudson, was executive producer of TV One’s “The Gospel of Music with Jeff Majors” for 5 seasons. He is currently enrolled in Graduate School at the University Of Miami to receive his Masters Degree in journalism.
